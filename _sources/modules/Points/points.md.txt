# Points

## `SetPoints`

Represents an ordered collection of points.

### Attributes

- `n` (int): The number of points.
- `dim` (int): The dimension of the points.
- `points` (numpy.ndarray): The coordinates of the points.
- `pos` (dict): A dictionary mapping point indices to coordinates.
- `centroid` (numpy.ndarray): The centroid of the points.

### Methods

#### `__init__(self, points, seed=None)`

Initializes a SetPoints object.

- **Parameters:**
    - `points` (numpy.ndarray): The coordinates of the points.
    - `seed` (int, optional): The seed for the random number generator.

#### `copy(self)`

Creates a copy of the SetPoints object.

#### `draw(self, ...)`

Draws the points.

#### `rotation(self, angle, degree=True)`

Rotates the points.

#### `scaling(self, scale)`

Scales the points.

#### `traslation(self, c)`

Translates the points.

#### `perturb(self, radius)`

Perturbs the points.
