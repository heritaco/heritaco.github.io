summary: 'This manuscript gives a normalized one-dimensional integral for the \(d\)-dimensional volume of the stepping-stone empty region defined by \(\lVert z-p\rVert^\alpha+\lVert z-q\rVert^\alpha\leq \ell_{p,q}^\alpha\) in \(\mathbb{R}^d\). It treats the normalized coordinate geometry, boundary parametrization, computational quadrature, anchor values at \(\alpha=1\) and \(\alpha=2\), the relative-neighborhood limit as \(\alpha\to\infty\), monotonicity, and universal bounds.'
formula: 'a_{d,\mathrm{SS}}(\alpha)=2\kappa_{d-1}\int_{2^{-1/\alpha}}^{1}y_\alpha(u)^{d-1}x_\alpha^{\prime}(u)\,du'
framing: "The manuscript is framed as a dimension-lift of a planar empty-region computation: symmetry reduces the d-dimensional stepping-stone region to a one-dimensional quadrature over transverse ball slices."

  - 'Shows anchor values at \(\alpha=1\) and \(\alpha=2\) and the relative-neighborhood limit as \(\alpha\to\infty\).'
